Know the History

Shimoga, officially known as Shivamogga, is a district in Karnataka, India, renowned for its rich history and cultural heritage, which significantly contribute to its tourism appeal. The region's historical narrative is shaped by the reigns of various dynasties, each leaving an indelible mark on its cultural and architectural landscape. The district's historical significance is further highlighted by its association with the Keladi Nayaka dynasty. Established by the Keladi rulers, Shimoga reached its zenith during the reign of Shivappa Nayaka in the 17th century. His rule is marked by the construction of the Shivappa Nayaka Palace, a notable heritage site in Shimoga city.

Adventures in Shivamogga

Lion and Tiger Safari, Water Adventures & Elephant Encounters Experience the thrill of the Lion and Tiger Safari, where you can witness majestic big cats roaming in a naturalistic habitat. For adventure seekers, Honnemaradu offers an array of exciting water activities such as kayaking, canoeing, and coracle rides on the scenic backwaters of the Sharavathi River. Don’t miss a visit to the Sakrebailu Elephant Camp, where you can observe and interact with elephants during their daily routines like bathing and feeding. These are just a few highlights—Shivamogga is home to many more unforgettable experiences that blend nature, wildlife, and adventure in one incredible destination.
